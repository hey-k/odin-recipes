Fried Rice

Description

This is a description of the food.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Protein of choice
  • evoo
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster Sauce

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs, cooking them just enough
  2. microwave the frozen vegetables for 1 minute, check it, and add another minute if necessary
  3. add your vegetables to the pan and cook
  4. add your protein
  5. rice
  6. about 2 spoons of soy sauce and 1 spoon of oyster sauce
  7. after it seems cooked and mixed well, add your eggs back to the pan
  8. add more soy sauce and oyster sauce to taste
  9. mix well
  10. serve
