Spam Fried Rice

Description

This is a quick and simple way to make spam fried rice. Since most of the ingredients are pre-cooked, it's very low prep.

Ingredients

  • 2 containers of pre-cooked ready-to-microwave rice
  • 3 Eggs
  • Frozen vegetables: California mix (peas, carrots, corn)
  • Spam - large size
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster Sauce

Steps

  1. Scramble the eggs, cooking them just enough and remove from pan
  2. Microwave the frozen vegetables for 1 minute, check it, and add another minute if necessary
  3. Add your vegetables to the pan
  4. Add diced spam to the pan
  5. Cook the vegetables and spam
  6. Add pre-cooked rice
  7. Add about 2 large spoonfuls of soy sauce and 1 spoonful of oyster sauce
  8. After it seems cooked and mixed well, add your cooked eggs
  9. Add more soy sauce and oyster sauce to taste
  10. Mix well
  11. Serve
