Tofu Salad

Description

This is a very simple way to make a tofu salad.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce or leafy green mix of choice
  • Firm tofu
  • Carrots
  • Cucumbers
  • Oriental salad dressing

Steps

  1. Press water out of tofu and cut into cubes
  2. Spray olive oil on tofu cubes and cook them in the air fryer
  3. Let the tofu cool
  4. Once cool, toss the tofu in the salad dressing
  5. Clean and slice carrots and cucumbers
  6. Mix everything together and add your dressing
