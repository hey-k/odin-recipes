Ham and Cheese Toast

Description

The most basic of sandwiches.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Cheese that melts well
  • Ham lunchmeat
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter just one side of 2 slices of bread
  2. Put one slice of bread butter side down in the pan
  3. Add slices of cheese and ham
  4. Put the other slice of bread on top, butter side up
  5. Cook the sandwich on very low heat
  6. Once the first side is browned and toasty, flip the sandwich
  7. Keep cooking in on low heat until the other side is also toasty
  8. Remove sandwich from the pan and enjoy!
